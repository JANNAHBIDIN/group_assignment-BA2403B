About our cafe

Itaewon Stall is located in Kota Bharu, Kelantan. It has two branches wich are in Lembah Sireh and Pengkala Chepa. This restaurant has a 3.8-star rating on Google, with 73 reviews. They offer Korean staples like sundubu jjigae (soft tofu stew), jajangmyeon (black bean noodles), and rabokki (spicy rice cakes).
